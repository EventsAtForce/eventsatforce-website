ABOUT US

FORCE-Forum Of REVA for Communication and Electronics, established in 2012, is a vibrant community exclusively for the students and faculty of School of Electronics and Communication Engineering, REVA University, Bengaluru.

WHAT WE DO

FORCE is a platform for students to showcase their talents, skills, and creativity. We conduct various events, workshops, and seminars to enhance the technical and soft skills of the students, fostering a dynamic community for students and faculty of REVA University.
